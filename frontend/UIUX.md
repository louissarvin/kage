# üåë Kage UI / UX Design System

Kage adopts a **Shadow-Centric UI** design approach inspired by **concealment, silence, and controlled presence**.  
The interface feels **intentional, restrained, and atmospheric** ‚Äî not loud, not decorative, but deep.

The design communicates power through **absence**, clarity through **contrast**, and trust through **precision**.

---

## Design Philosophy

- **Power through restraint**
- **Visibility by contrast, not brightness**
- **Silence as authority**
- **Nothing unnecessary, nothing accidental**

The UI should feel like:

> something important is happening ‚Äî quietly.

Users should sense **control and confidence**, not excitement or spectacle.

---

## Design Style

### Primary Style
- Shadow-first UI
- Dark atmospheric surfaces
- High-contrast focus zones

### Secondary Accent
- Subtle shadow layering
- Controlled light reveals (no glass, no heavy blur)

Instead of glass effects, Kage uses **depth through darkness** ‚Äî overlapping layers, soft occlusion, and implied boundaries.

---

## Color System

- **Primary Background:** Deep charcoal / near-black  
- **Secondary Surfaces:** Soft graphite / dark slate  
- **Primary Text:** Off-white / muted silver  
- **Accent Color:** Desaturated green or cold steel gray  
  *(used sparingly for emphasis or state changes)*  
- **Warning / Attention:** Muted ember / dark amber  

The palette avoids pure black‚Äìwhite extremes to preserve softness and long-session comfort.

---

## Typography

- **Headings:** Sharp sans-serif (Inter / Satoshi / Neue Montreal)
- **Body Text:** Neutral sans-serif with high legibility
- **Numbers & Data:** Slightly condensed or monospaced variant

Typography should feel:
- deliberate
- technical
- quiet

No playful curves. No decorative strokes.

---

## Core UI Components

### 1. Primary Interface Surface

- Dark layered panels
- Separation achieved via depth, not borders
- Focused content zones illuminated by contrast

---

### 2. Detail View / Focus Screen

- One dominant focal element
- Supporting elements recede into shadow
- Actions feel intentional, not accidentally discoverable

The UI guides attention **without explanation**.

---

### 3. Overlay / Explanation Layer

- Darkened backdrop
- Slightly elevated panel
- Clear hierarchy, no visual noise

Overlays feel like:

> stepping deeper into the system ‚Äî not popping out of it.

---

### 4. Status & History Views

- Vertical flow
- Time-based grouping
- Clear distinction between active and inactive states

Inactive elements **fade into darkness**, not removal.

---

## Interaction Principles

- No sudden motion
- No aggressive animations
- Transitions are slow, subtle, and purposeful

Every interaction should feel:
- controlled
- predictable
- calm

---
